Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use our website or book a laundry service, we may collect:

  • Personal information: Name, phone number, email address, and delivery address
  • Service details: Items being laundered, preferred branch, pickup dates, and special instructions
  • Usage data: Browser type, IP address, pages visited, and interaction patterns (via Google Analytics)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill laundry service requests
  • To contact you about your order status via WhatsApp or phone
  • To improve our website and services (analytics)
  • To send updates about new services or promotions (with your consent)

3. Data Protection

Your personal information is stored securely in Google Sheets, accessible only to authorized RESAYLE staff. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or loss.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Sheets: To store and manage customer orders and data
  • Google Analytics: To track website usage and improve the user experience
  • WhatsApp: To send order confirmations and updates

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

5. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies. Google Analytics may set cookies to track your browsing behavior. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal obligations)
  • Opt out of marketing communications
